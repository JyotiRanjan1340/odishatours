Popular Odisha Destinations

Bhubaneswar

Temple city featuring Lingaraj Temple, Khandagiri & Udayagiri caves, and vibrant heritage walks.

Puri

Famous for Jagannath Temple, golden beaches, and the annual Rath Yatra festival.

Konark

UNESCO Sun Temple, a marvel of Kalinga architecture and history.

Chilika Lake

Asia's largest brackish water lagoon, famous for birds, dolphins, and boat rides.

Similipal National Park

Tigers, elephants, waterfalls, and lush forests for eco-tourism and safaris.

Daringbadi

Odisha's very own hill station, pine forests, and coffee gardens.
