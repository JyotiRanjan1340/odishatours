Popular Odisha Destinations

🏛️

Bhubaneswar

The Temple City with over 500 ancient temples, including Lingaraj Temple, Mukteshwar Temple, and modern attractions like Udayagiri caves.

🏖️

Puri

Sacred city home to Lord Jagannath Temple, pristine beaches, and the famous Rath Yatra festival. Perfect for spiritual and beach experiences.

☀️

Konark

Marvel at the UNESCO World Heritage Sun Temple, an architectural masterpiece carved from black granite in the 13th century.

🐦

Chilika Lake

Asia's largest brackish water lagoon, famous for migratory birds, dolphins, and scenic islands. Perfect for nature lovers and photographers.

🐅

Similipal National Park

One of India's largest tiger reserves with waterfalls, tribal villages, and diverse wildlife including tigers, elephants, and leopards.

🏔️

Eastern Ghats

Hill stations like Daringbadi and Koraput offering cool climate, coffee plantations, and breathtaking valley views.
